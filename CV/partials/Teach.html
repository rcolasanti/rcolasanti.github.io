Dr Ric Colasanti CV


I was lucky enough to discover that I really enjoy computer programming, so on the back of that I got a Ph.D. from the University of Sheffield and my partner Diane and I have spent the last 10 years doing post doc placements in the US and Australia. Having just graduated from Cardiff University with a PGCE (Post-Compulsory), I now want to pass my knowledge and enthusiasm onto others..

Employment

I have had a varied and exciting career as a programmer. I have worked for a movie promotion company writing screensavers and web games for films from Star Trek to Beavis and Butthead. I have been the senior developer for a biotech start up and created three patented drug discovery programs I have not have been as excited about the state of computing as I am now. I have been lucky enought to do my teaching practice at the School of Computing at the University of Cardiff. I contributed to the teaching of three modules each at the forefront of moden computing.
