Dr Ric Colasanti


I was lucky enough to discover that I really enjoy computer programming, so on the back of that I got a Ph.D. from the University of Sheffield and my partner Diane and I have spent the last 10 years doing post doc placements in the US and Australia. I am now in the seaside town of Barry Island in Wales, where we can get a proper cup of tea and I have just graduated from Cardiff University with a PGCE (Post-Compulsory), so that I can now to pass my knowledge and enthusiasm onto others
